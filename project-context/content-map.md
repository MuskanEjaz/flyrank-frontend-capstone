# Content Map

## Home
- Short introduction with name, role, and a clear portfolio point of view.
- Keep the initial impression polished and calm.

## About
- Share background, study focus, and the kind of frontend work being built.
- Keep the tone grounded in real experience and current learning.

## Projects
- Feature case studies and product work with concise summaries.
- Use verified details first and leave placeholders where evidence is missing.

## Skills
- Group practical frontend strengths such as UI implementation, responsive design, and product thinking.
- Keep lists focused and easy to scan.

## Experience
- Capture relevant work, study, and project history in a factual and minimal format.
- Avoid invented dates or accomplishments.

## Contact
- Provide a simple contact path for collaboration or follow-up.
- Keep the section direct and welcoming.
